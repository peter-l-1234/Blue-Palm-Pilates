About Us

Welcome to Blue Palm Pilates, where we focus on building strength, flexibility, and balance through Pilates. Our experienced instructor is here to guide you on your fitness journey. Having practised yoga for over thirty years, a chance encounter on holiday with a Pilates teacher sparked an interest, and within a year of that inspiring meeting Linda was a qualified YMCA L3 Pilates Practitioner running her own classes.
